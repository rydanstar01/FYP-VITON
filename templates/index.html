Virtual Try On

Virtual Try On Project helps consumers to try the clothes virtually without physically buying them. The consumers can virtually try the desired cloth on their body by the mode of this website. The consumer will upload the picture his/her along with the desired cloth which he/she wants to try-on on this website. Then, the pictures will be sent to our model for processing the pictures, segmenting the key body points, wrapping the cloth, fusing the content and generating the final output. This output image with desired cloth wrapped on the consumer's body will be dispalyed on the website.

Need


1) Return rates are increasing due to consumer's not being satisfied with their choice of clothes.

2) Consumer's dont have much option to try the cloth on themself without physically visiting the store.

3) Due to limited choices and ambiguous decision making, consumer is unable to buy person desired clothes.

Model

Our model consists of following modules

Semantic Generation Module

Generates the mask of the body parts and the mask of the warped clothing regions via semantic segmentation

Clothes Wraping Module

Warp target clothing image according to the warped clothing mask

Content Fusion Module

Integrates the information from previous modules to adaptively determine the generation or preservation of the distinct human parts in output synthesized image
